Vibrant Vinyls


At Vibrant Vinyls, we believe music is more than something you listen to—it's something you feel. Based in the heart of Dublin, we’re a community-driven vinyl reseller dedicated to keeping the warmth, nostalgia, and culture of record collecting alive.


We stock a wide selection of brand-new releases, from modern chart-toppers to limited-edition pressings. But what makes us truly unique is our passion for giving pre-loved records a second life. Through donations and trade-ins from the local community, we curate an ever-changing collection of second-hand gems, each with its own story and history.


Giving back is core to who we are. Whether it’s hosting community music events, supporting local artists, or partnering with neighbourhood initiatives, we’re committed to making Dublin’s music scene more vibrant—one record at a time.


At Vibrant Vinyls, everyone is welcome. Whether you’re a long-time collector, a new listener, or someone simply looking for your next favourite sound, we’re here to help you discover the music that moves you.


Spin something new. Rediscover something old. Experience the community behind the music.
